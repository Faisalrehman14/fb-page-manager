Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Page Inbox ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our customer support inbox application for Facebook Pages ("Service").

2. Information We Collect

When you use our Service, we may collect the following information:

  • Facebook Profile Information: Your name and user ID when you authenticate with Facebook.
  • Facebook Page Information: Names, IDs, and profile pictures of Pages you administer.
  • Conversation Data: Messages between your Pages and customers, including sender information, message content, and timestamps.
  • Session Data: Temporary session identifiers stored in cookies for authentication purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Authenticate you and maintain your session
  • Display your Facebook Pages and their conversations
  • Enable you to read and respond to customer messages
  • Provide the customer support functionality of the Service

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Access Tokens: Facebook access tokens are stored only in server-side sessions and are never exposed to the browser or logged.
  • Session Security: We use HTTP-only, secure cookies with appropriate SameSite attributes.
  • No Permanent Storage: We do not permanently store your Facebook data, messages, or conversations. All data is fetched in real-time from Facebook's API.
  • Encryption: All data transmission occurs over HTTPS.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is only transmitted between your browser, our servers, and Facebook's API as necessary to provide the Service.

6. Facebook Platform Compliance

Our use of Facebook data complies with:

  • Facebook Platform Terms
  • Facebook Developer Policies
  • Facebook Community Standards

We only request permissions necessary for the Service's functionality: pages_show_list, pages_read_engagement, and pages_messaging.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disconnect: Revoke access at any time using the "Disconnect" button, which removes your session and revokes Facebook permissions.
  • Access: Request information about what data we have processed.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of any cached session data.

8. Data Retention

Session data is automatically deleted when you disconnect or after 24 hours of inactivity. We do not retain Facebook data beyond your active session.

9. Cookies

We use a single session cookie (fb_inbox_session) for authentication. This cookie is essential for the Service to function and cannot be disabled while using the Service.

10. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@example.com
