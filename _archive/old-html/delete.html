Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: March 2026

How to Delete Your Data

Page Inbox does not store any personal data permanently. All session data is automatically deleted when you log out or after 24 hours of inactivity.

To Remove App Access

  1. Log in to your Facebook Settings
  2. Click on Apps and Websites
  3. Find Page Inbox and click Remove
  4. This will revoke all permissions and delete any associated data

Disconnect from Within the App

You can also disconnect directly from the app by clicking the "Disconnect" button in the dashboard. This immediately revokes all Facebook permissions and clears your session data.
